## Guidelines to Submit a Guest Blog

Please follow these editorial requirements:

* Minimum **1000+ words**.
* Must be **original**, **plagiarism‑free**, and written for an intelligent audience.
* Provide clear headings, subheadings, and bullet lists.
* Content must be helpful, data-driven, and reader‑focused.
* Include 1–2 copyright‑free images or relevant stock charts.
* Short **author bio** (2–3 lines).

Submit your article as a **Google Doc** or **Markdown file** to:
