### A Big Problem for a Little Boy

When Lionel was 10 years old, doctors diagnosed him with Growth Hormone Deficiency (GHD). This meant his body wasn't producing enough hormones to grow properly. He was much smaller than other kids his age.

The condition caused concerns that he might not grow tall enough to become a professional footballer. This was a devastating blow for a boy whose entire life revolved around the sport. Imagine hearing such news at that age.

The treatment for GHD involved daily hormone injections, which were very expensive. His family, already living modestly, struggled to afford the high costs. They tried everything to find a way.

Newell's Old Boys initially promised to help cover the medical expenses. However, they soon backed out, leaving the family in a difficult situation. This was a huge setback for them.

River Plate, another big Argentine club, also showed interest in Messi but couldn't commit to paying for his treatment. The future looked uncertain for the young star. You can feel the tension and worry.

### The Search for a Solution

The financial burden of Messi's treatment was immense, costing around $900 per month. This was a fortune for his working-class family in Argentina. They felt like they were running out of options.

His father, Jorge, was desperately looking for a club that would pay for his son's medical bills. They knew Lionel had exceptional talent, but his condition was a significant barrier. Every door seemed to close.

### The Historic Napkin Contract

In September 2000, when Lionel was 13, his father arranged a trial with FC Barcelona in Spain. The club's sporting director, Carles Rexach, was there to watch him play. He was immediately captivated by Messi's talent.

Rexach famously didn't have any paper on hand to formalize an agreement. So, he wrote a contract on a paper napkin right there and then. This legendary napkin promised Messi a contract if he moved to Spain.

The napkin contract is now a famous artifact in football history. It symbolized Barcelona's immediate belief in the small Argentine boy. You can find images of this important document online.

This quick decision shows just how extraordinary Messi's talent was. Rexach knew he couldn't let such a gifted player slip away. It was a moment of pure football magic.

Barcelona agreed to pay for all of Lionel's growth hormone treatment. This was the breakthrough his family had been desperately searching for. It was a life-changing offer.
