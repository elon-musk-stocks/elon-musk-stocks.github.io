#### A Health Scare: The Growth Hormone Deficiency

However, not all was easy in Messi's early life. At the age of 10, he faced a serious challenge. He was diagnosed with a growth hormone deficiency. This meant his body wasn't producing enough hormones to help him grow properly. This news was devastating for his family.
