### The Humble Beginnings of a Legend

Lionel Messi grew up in a place called Rosario, Argentina, a lively city full of football lovers. From a very young age, he loved to play football more than anything else. ### A Challenge That Could Have Ended His Dream

When Messi was just ten years old, his family received some very difficult news. This news was heartbreaking, as it meant he might not grow tall enough to become a professional footballer. You can imagine how scary that must have been for him and his family.

The treatment for this condition was very expensive, costing hundreds of dollars every month. His local club, Newell's Old Boys, offered to help, but it wasn't enough to cover all the costs. This was one of the biggest **messi struggles and success** moments he would face.
